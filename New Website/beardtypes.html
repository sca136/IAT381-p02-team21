Beard Styles

Pointy

Pointy beard is mostly like any normal beard. However the unique look of a pointy beard is when it sharpens up as it goes downwards your chin. All the hair will eventually point to one direction a few milimeters under your chin where in programming it is your (0,0) origin of cartesian coordinate.
Psycho

Psycho beard is best accomodated with a psychotic look on your face. If this is the type of look that you think you own, then psycho beard styling is the best way to go. It is as if the beard enhances the fear that is implicited to other people around you.
Goatee

Goatee beard style is when you want to look as if you're opening your mouth when it isn't. It is really great for presenting to public because it always seem that you're always talking even though you're on a pause.
Insane

An insane beard is technically psycho beard version 2.0. This beard type changes every hour depending on your mood and current expression. This beard style will be able to apply and communicate what you're feeling inside you to other people, hence the name. Completely insane... dude.
